Simulator for the Paris Saclay Autonomous Car Race

To accelerate the development of algorithms for autonomous cars, several teams use simulators, either developed internally (in Python or under Matlab), or based on existing simulators (AirSim, Webots, etc.).

Sharing different simulator experiences is encouraged to help new teams get started faster, and even to hold competitions on the simulator before the official race date.

For the 2023-2024 season, a Webots 2023b project with a typical car, a basic controller in C and one in Python, as well as elements of the official track, is available on Git, in the archive Simulateur_CoVAPSy_Webots2023b
